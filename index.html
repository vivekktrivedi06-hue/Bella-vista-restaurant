Meet the Chef

Marco Ferretti — a life spent chasing flavour.

Born in Bologna, trained in Modena and Paris, Marco brings three decades of restlessness to the Bella Vista kitchen. His food is quiet, precise and unmistakably Italian.

Our Story

A table set for generations.

Bella Vista opened its doors in 1987 as a tiny six-table trattoria on the corner of Via del Sole. Almost forty years later, we're still in the same building — the copper pans hang from the same beams, the sourdough starter is still called Gino, and every guest is still welcomed like family.

What has changed is the ambition. We source olive oil from a single grove in Puglia, our flour from a stone-mill in Umbria, and our wine list runs more than 240 references — with a fierce love for small, biodynamic producers.
